We Grind Our Whole Wheat Fresh Everyday!

Our purpose and passion is to provide you and your family with the freshest and most flavorful baked goods you’ve ever tasted. Visit our bakery for a free slice of fresh bread or a taste of our legendary sweets. Enjoy yourself and bring your children. We’re a locally owned and community focused neighboorhood bakery.

Because freshly milled whole wheat tastes phenomenal, and fresh whole grain bread is life-enhancing. We use premium wheat bought from family-owned farms located in the Golden Triangle region of Montana -- the finest wheat growing region in the world.
